This is an online project inspired by Kennedy Browne's Milton Friedman and the Wonder of the Free Market Pencil artwork.

My name is Ekaterina Pavlenko, I am a software developer from Russia.

When I have seen this artwork for the first time at General Rehearsal in Moscow, I couldn't stop thinking about creating a digital version of it and giving people possibility to watch the same metamorphoses happening to other text.
